The Idaho Argonaut student newspaper is available as a [digital collection](https://www.lib.uidaho.edu/digital/argonaut/). Spec also keep physical copies in Cage 1, Rows 34-35.

Currently, the Argonaut is mostly an online publication. A physical paper is only published twice a semester. When a print is made, collect a copy to place in the box with the corresponding year.
